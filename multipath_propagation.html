How it works: The UE (User Equipment) transmits a signal that reaches the RU (Radio Unit) through multiple paths. The Line-of-Sight (LOS) path is direct, while other paths bounce off buildings and objects (reflections and scattering). Each path has different delay (distance traveled), phase (time offset), and Angle of Arrival (AoA). Multiple antennas can distinguish between paths based on their arrival angles, enabling beamforming and spatial multiplexing.

Click on the canvas to add/move scatterers and see how paths change!
