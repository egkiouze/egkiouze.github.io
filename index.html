Summary

Eleni Gkiouzepi (Graduate Student Member, IEEE) received a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens in 2015 and an M.Sc. in Applied Statistics from Athens University of Economics and Business in 2019. She is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Communications and Information Theory Chair, Technical University of Berlin.

From 2020 to 2021, she was with Université Grenoble Alpes and worked in the field of privacy and security. Her research interests include cell-free massive MIMO and message passing algorithms.
